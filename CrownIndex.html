About Crown

We are Crown Cosmetics which was established in 2014. We are a fast growing company that has made over 1,000,000 sales in the past year. We are proud to have vegan and cruelty free products. We have perfected our lipstick formula. Our lipsticks can last all day! They will not dry out your lips like other brands. We are also working on new and exciting eyeshadow palettes that push the envelope and are different and unique. We are very affordable and have various prices ranges from very low to more high end. Crown Cosmetics is also very inclusive and encourages all of the beauty community to make the most out of our makeup.
